### What's new: Bootstrap Quick Start

The sign-in dialog now has a 4th option:

    ○  App Link          [Recommended]
    ○  URL scheme
    ○  QR Code
    ○  Bootstrap Quick Start         ← new

Tapping "Bootstrap Quick Start":

1. Generates a fresh identity key pair locally on the device.
2. Generates a fresh delegate key pair locally.
3. Signs the user in immediately — no third-party app, no network account, no blank screen.
4. Shows a content feed right away (the developer's network, as a starting point).

The user can rate, comment, follow, and block immediately. All activity is signed with their
local delegate key. If they later install the ONE-OF-US.NET identity app and create a real
identity, they can claim their delegate key and all their activity is preserved.

The orange key icon in the top-right corner indicates bootstrap mode and, when tapped, explains
the temporary nature of the identity and the path to graduating to a full identity.

---

### How to review/try the app

Simply launch the app and tap "Bootstrap Quick Start". You will be signed in immediately and
can explore the full UI — browse content, rate, comment, follow — without any other app.

---

### The original sign-in methods (still present)

For users who already have the ONE-OF-US.NET identity app:

- **App Link** (recommended on iPhone): launches the identity app directly to transmit keys.
- **URL scheme** (keymeid://): same, via a custom URL scheme.
- **QR Code**: scan sign-in parameters with the identity app on another device.
