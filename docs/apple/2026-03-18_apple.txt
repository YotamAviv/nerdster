Guideline 1.2 - Safety - User-Generated Content

Issue Description
We found in our review that the app includes user-generated content but does not have all the required precautions.

Next Steps
Implement the following:
- Require that users agree to terms (EULA) making it clear there is no tolerance for objectionable content.
- A method for filtering objectionable content.
- A mechanism for users to flag objectionable content.
- A mechanism for users to block abusive users (notifying the developer and removing content instantly).
- Developer must act within 24 hours by removing content and ejecting the offending user.
