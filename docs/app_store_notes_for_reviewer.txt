NOTES FOR REVIEWER
==================

To try the app without the ONE-OF-US.NET identity app:

1. Launch the Nerdster.
2. Tap "Bootstrap Quick Start" (4th option in the sign-in dialog).
3. You are immediately signed in and can browse, rate, comment, and follow.

No other app is required. No account creation. No network connection to a third-party service.

The orange key icon in the top-right indicates bootstrap mode. Tapping it shows an explanation
of what bootstrap mode is and how a user can later connect their own identity.
