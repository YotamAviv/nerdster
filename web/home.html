nerdster.org

The Nerd'ster!

  • A demo / reference implementation / proof of concept of the paradigm, works with the ONE-OF-US.NET phone app.
    • Identify yourself using your public key to use trusted content from your Point of View.
    • Sign in with a delegate key to publish your contributions as yourself.
  • Show off the crypto (statements, signatures, identity keys, delegate keys, identity conflicts, auditing tools, etc..)
  • Purist: naked, fully open
  • Dime-a-dozen forum like web app to submit links, like, comment, and facilitate following others...
    • All the stuff we could do 30 years ago, but different (not the killer app ;)
    • Puny and weak, won't handle content from thousands around you (but you can shift Pov).

The bigger picture is at https://one-of-us.net
